Diaries of Tom Turley - First entry

About mother

June 6, 2019

Mother was crying again. She tried to be quiet, but I heard. I will never get used to the sound. Why can’t they just be happy? They must have found each other for a reason? I wonder if they had it better before me.

Father came to say goodnight. He sat on my bed side and didn’t say a thing. He just looked through the window, into the distance. I asked him if he would leave again, and he frowned.
